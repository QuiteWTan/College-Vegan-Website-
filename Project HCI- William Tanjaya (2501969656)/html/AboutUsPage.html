History

VOrgreens was first conceptualized in 2017 over a simple discussion with friends about the future food.
Fast forward 5 years, what began as a tasteful idea in Jakarta, Indonesia has now become a mouth-watering plant-based food serving restaurant. VOrgreens was founded on the premise that tasty meals and natural plant-based ingredients should go hand-in-hand for environmental sustainability.

OUR FARM
Doing hardwork in the farm so to give the best quality ingredient in our reastaurant for the best experience people can have and taste.
"Farming looks mighty easy when your plow is a pencil, and you're a thousand miles from the crop field"

What Are We Trying To Achieve?

Our Vision

We open this plant based food restaurant so that one day it would be the best plant based food serving restaurant in the whole Indonesia, and also that plant based food are widely approachable and affordable for all the people in Indonesia.

Our Mission

We hope to open people's eyes of the plant based food cuisine that we proudly make and serve through our hardwork to gather fresh ingredient and make people know that the plant based food of ours are worth to try.
